About

Jelly Jumper is an South African childrens band from Durban, Kwa-Zulu Natal. The band's current lineup comprises vocalist/rhythm guitarist/keyboardist Jessie James, lead guitarist Brad Lawson, bassist David Patterson, DJ/turntablist Joe Doe and drummer Rob Burns, all of whom are founding members. Vocalists Mark Garfield and Chester Stevens are former members of the band.

Classfied as childrens band, performing arts at kids parties as well as kids concerts at various parks and other events for kids and youth.

  • Youth concerts

Education

  • Degree in teaching music - University of Music Year 2020
